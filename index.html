General



I am a sociolinguist who primarily studies language variation and change.

My interests lie in variation that stems from situations of ethnic contact in the US as well as the variation related to social identities, institutional ideologies, and the hegemonic structures of race in the US.

Dissertation: Race, ethnicity, and social groups: Social and linguistic causes of language variation among 6th grade students at a primarily Black South Florida middle school
